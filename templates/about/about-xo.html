{% trans 'What We Believe In' %}

  1. {% blocktrans trimmed %} We believe everyone can make their own fresh and great tasting coffee at home or at work as long as the beans are grown and processed at the best farms, and freshly roasted with love and care. That’s why we source only the finest and always send out your coffee to you within 1 week* of roasting. Don’t get too startled when that heavenly aroma hits you! {% endblocktrans %}

  2. {% blocktrans trimmed %} We’re big on sustainability so we’ve adopted the Direct Trade model to give you traceability of our coffees and ethical choices. Our farmers receive a bigger share of the final retail value and this is just one of the ways we show our appreciation for their skills and hard work as growing and processing specialty coffee is a real art. We aren’t 100% Direct Trade yet, but we’re definitely getting there. {% endblocktrans %}

  3. {% blocktrans trimmed %} Although we think you’re likely to get hooked on us, we know commitment can be a scary thing. We won’t tie you to any contract — you can edit, pause or cancel your subscription at any time, with zero commitment. And since change is only natural, you can update your profile so we can recommend you a coffee better suited to your needs and preferences. {% endblocktrans %}
